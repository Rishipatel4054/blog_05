Emerging Technologies

How artificial intelligence is transforming the world

https://www.brookings.edu/articles/how-artificial-intelligence-is-transforming-the-world/ April 24, 2018

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a multifaceted tool that allows individuals to reevaluate how we combine information, analyze data, and use the resulting insights to improve decision making. Already, AI is revolutionizing all facets of human life. In this report, Darrell West and John Allen cover the application of AI in a variety of fields, the challenges it faces in development, and suggestions for maximizing AI's potential while upholding core human values.
